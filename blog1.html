⚡ 5 Common Electrical Mistakes at Home

  1. Overloading sockets: Plugging too many devices into one outlet can cause overheating and fires.
  2. Using damaged cords: Exposed wires are dangerous. Always replace damaged cables.
  3. Wet hands near electricity: Never touch switches or plugs with wet hands — this can cause shocks.
  4. Poor DIY wiring: Leave complex wiring to professionals. Mistakes can be deadly.
  5. Ignoring buzzing or burning smells: These are signs of serious problems. Turn off power and call an expert.
