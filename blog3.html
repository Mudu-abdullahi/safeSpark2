🔌 Safe Use of Plugs and Sockets

  • Always fully insert plugs — loose plugs can cause sparks.
  • Never yank cords from sockets — unplug by holding the plug, not the cord.
  • Do not overload multi-sockets — use certified power strips with circuit breakers.
  • Teach children to stay away from sockets — use plastic safety covers if needed.
  • Regularly check for signs of damage or burning — replace old sockets.
